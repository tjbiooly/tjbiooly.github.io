About TJ Biology Olympiad


TJ Biology Olympiad meets in Room 22 every Friday during B block of 8th period, and after school on Fridays in Room 54 from 4-6 PM. Join us for lectures during 8th period or practice after school! Throughout the year, TJ Biology Olympiad participates in the USA Biology Olympiad and University of Toronto National Biology competitions. We will also be hosting 2 in-house contests, the Biology Olympiad Winter Contest (BOWC) and Spring Contest (BOSC).

Biology Olympiad Winter Contest (BOWC)


The BOWC is a 30 question multiple-choice contest and will take place during 8th period on Friday, December 7th, 2018.


Topics covered on the BOWC will include all of the lectures that take place before the contest. Over winter break, the officers will grade the contests and post the results online. An answer key with explanations to all the questions will also be posted. There will be three divisions in the contest (IBET, AP Bio, and post-AP Bio). The top two winners in each division will receive prizes.



USA BIOLOGY OLYMPIAD


The USA Biology Olympaid (USABO) selects talented students from around the country and prepares them for the International Biology Olympiad (IBO). The USABO is made up of three rounds:


Open Exam -- Anyone can take this exam. It is a 50-minute multiple choice exam and will be held during A and B block of an 8th period (you will be signed up for both). This year, the open exam will be held on February 15th, 2019 (tentative).


Semifinal Exam -- USABO semifinalists take this exam. Semifinalist qualifications are usually the top 10% of scorers or about the top 500 people on the Open Exam. It is a 120-minute exam that has multiple choice, short answer, and essay questions. The semifinal exam will be held on March 22nd, 2019 (tentative).


Finals and Camp -- The top 20 scorers on the Semifinal Exam are eligible to go to USABO camp. Finalists prepare for the IBO and the final team is chosen to represent the USA at IBO. The national finalists will be posted on the USABO website by April 10th, 2019. The national finals are from June 22 to July 6 (tentative).


For more information, see the USABO website at http://www.usabo-trc.org/.



UNIVERSITY OF TORONTO NATIONAL BIOLOGY COMPETITION


The University of Toronto National Biology Competition (Toronto Exam) is a 75-minute exam with 50 multiple choice questions. The exam covers general high school biology. This year's Toronto Exam will be held on Thursday, April 25, 2019 (subject to change).


For more information, see the University of Toronto website at https://biocomp.utoronto.ca/.
