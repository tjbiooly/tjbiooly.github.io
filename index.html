Our purpose

USABO

 

Our primary aim is to provide a conducive learning community for advancing the knowledge of Biology in our school. Our officers put in hard work to make sure Biology isn't just one of those high school classes. However, a lot of our club is focused on preparing for the USA's Biology Olympiad competition which is held in February each year. In addition, we prepare for a few smaller ones like our annual winter and spring contests.
